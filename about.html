Vilupt Janjatiyan (Extinct Species)

Vilupt janjatiyan woh prajatiyan hain jo dharti par pehle thi, lekin ab nahi rahi. Yeh prakriti ke santulan ke liye ek sabak hain ki hume apni dharti aur jeev jantu ki raksha karni chahiye. Vilupt prajatiyon ka karan manav kriyaayein, prakritik badlav, ya habitat ka nuksaan ho sakta hai.

Kyun Hoti Hai Vilupti?

🌿 Jungle aur prakritik habitat ka katna
🏹 Zyada shikar (hunting)
☣️ Pollution aur chemical contamination
🌍 Climate change aur prakritik aapdaayein

Kuch Mashhoor Vilupt Prajatiyan

"Vilupt prajatiyon ki kahani humein yaad dilati hai ki prakriti ke saath hamara rishta kitna naazuk hai aur hume uski raksha karni chahiye."

Hum Kya Kar Sakte Hain?

🌱 Vanaspati aur janglon ki raksha karna
🚯 Pollution kam karna
🦁 Wildlife protection areas banwana
📢 Awareness badhana aur hunting par rok lagana

Aise kadam uthaakar hum apni dharti ko aur jeevant aur sundar bana sakte hain, taaki aane wali naslein bhi iska laabh utha saken.
