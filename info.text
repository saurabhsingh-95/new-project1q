        <p class="pera">
            🔎 Huia :
            Vigyanik Naam: Heteralocha acutirostris
            Paya jaata tha: New Zealand
            IUCN Status: Extinct (vilupt)
            Vishesh pehchaan:
            Nar aur maada ke chonch alag-alag size ke hote the.
            Nar ki chonch seedhi hoti thi jabki maada ki lambi aur tedhi hoti thi.
            Kaale pankh aur gaal ke paas narangi rang ke "wattle" (latakte hue mass ke tukde).
            Aakhri baar dekha gaya: 1907 ke aaspaas
            Vilupti ka kaaran:
            Manav shikaar
            Vanon ka katav
            Videshi prajatiyon ka aakraman (jaise ki chuhe aur billi)
            🌿 Vishesh baat:
            Huia ko Maori logon ke liye ek pavitra pakshi maana jaata tha, aur uske pankh ko aadar ke roop mein pehna
            jaata tha.
        </p>

        <p class="pera">
            🐦 Great Hornbill (Buceros bicornis)
            🔹 Vigyanik Naam: Buceros bicornis
            🔹 Parivaar: Bucerotidae
            🔹 Paye jaate hain:
            India (Western Ghats, North-East)
            ,Nepal,Bhutan,
            ,Myanmar,Thailand, Malaysia
            🔍 Pehchaan:
            Badi aur rangin chonch ke upar ek helmet jaisa ubhra hua dhacha hota hai, jise casque kehte hain.
            Sar pe peela aur kala rang hota hai.
            Pankhon ke siron par safed dhari hoti hai.
            Lambi poonch aur majboot pair.
            🌳 Aawaas (Habitat):
            Tropical aur subtropical rainforest (ghane jungle)
            Zyada tar unchai par paaye jaate hain.
            🍇 Khaana (Diet):
            Phal (specially figs)
            Chhote janwar, insects, aur kabhi-kabhi chhoti chidiyaayein bhi
            👪 Jeevan Shailee:
            Maada ande dene ke baad ghonsle mein band ho jaati hai.
            Nar usse bahar se khaana deta hai jab tak bachche nikal nahi jaate.
        </p>

        <p class="pera">
            Spix's Macaw ke baare mein jankari:
            Vigyanik Naam: Cyanopsitta spixii
            Parivaar: Psittacidae (तोते का कुल)
            Paya jaata tha:
            Brazil ke dry forest (specially near Rio São Francisco)
            🔍 Pehchaan:
            Sundar neela rang ka sharir
            Sir par halka neela aur greyish shade
            Majboot aur tedhi kaali chonch
            Bada aur akarshak rang-roop
            🍃 Aawaas (Habitat):
            Gallery forest aise jungle jo nadiyon ke kinare hote hain
            Specially Brazil ke northeast region mein
            🥭 Khaana (Diet):
            Beej, phal, phool aur kuch-kuch ped ke gude (sap) par bhi nirbhar
            ⚠ IUCN Status:
            Extinct in the Wild (prakriti mein vilupt)
            Lekin kuch captivity mein (bandhan mein) bache hue hain aur unhe wapas jungle mein chhodne ki koshish ho
            rahi hai
            Vilupti ke kaaran:
            Pedon ka katav
            Ilegally pakad kar vyapar (bird trade)
            Prakritik aawaas ka vinaash
            🛡 Conservation efforts:
            Breeding programs
            Reintroduction (dobara jungle mein chhodne ka kaam)
            Brazil sarkar aur kai wildlife NGOs milkar kaam kar rahe hain
            Yeh pakshi "Rio" movie ke Blue Macaw se bhi prerit hai – jo isi prajati par adharit tha.</p>
