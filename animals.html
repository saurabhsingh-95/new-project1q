Woolly Mammoth 🦣

Naam: Woolly Mammoth (Hindi: Balonwala Haathi).
Varg: Yeh haathi ke purvaj the.
Awas: Siberia, Europe aur North America ke tundra regions mein rehte the.
Aahar: Yeh herbivore the – ghaas, paudhe, jhaadiyaan khate the.
Visheshta: Inke moote baal aur lambe, ghumaavdaar daant unhe thand se bachate the.
Vilupti: Lagbhag 4000 saal pehle jalvayu parivartan aur manav shikar se yeh vilupt ho gaye.

The Asiatic Cheetah 🐆

Naam: Asiatic Cheetah (Bharatiya Chita).
Awas: Bharat, Iran aur Arab ke dry plains (ab mainly Iran mein).
Aahar: Deer jaise small mammals ka shikar karta hai.
Visheshta: Duniya ka sabse tezi se daudne wala prani (~100 km/h).
Status: Critically Endangered – Iran mein sirf kuch hi bache hain.
Sankat: Habitat loss, hunting aur genetic diversity ki kami.

Giant Ground Sloth 🦣

Naam: Giant Ground Sloth.
Awas: South aur North America mein paaya jaata tha.
Aakar: 4 meter lamba aur 4 tonne tak wajan.
Aahar: Paudhe aur patte (herbivore).
Visheshta: Yeh zameen par rehta tha, unlike modern sloths.
Vilupti: 10,000 saal pehle, climate change aur manav shikar se.

Chipangi 🐵

Naam: Lion-Tailed Macaque (Chipangi).
Awas: Western Ghats, Bharat.
Pehchaan: Uski poonch sher ke jaise mane se ghiri hoti hai.
Aahar: Omnivore – phal, patte, kiṭ, keede.
Status: Endangered – forest loss se sankat mein.
Vyavhaar: Group mein rehte hain jisme mahila pradhan hoti hai.

Golden Lion Tamarin 🐒

Naam: Golden Lion Tamarin.
Awas: Brazil ke coastal rainforests.
Pehchaan: Golden baalon aur sher jaise mane wale bandar.
Aahar: Fruits, insects, chhoti reptiles.
Status: Endangered – habitat destruction ke kaaran.
Visheshta: Diurnal prani hain – din bhar active rehte hain.

Indri

Naam: Indri – Madagascar ka sabse bada lemur.
Awas: Eastern Madagascar ke rainforest mein.
Aahar: Patte aur chhoti daaliyaan (herbivore).
Visheshta: Bahut tez pukaar karta hai, jo km tak sunai deti hai.
Status: Critically Endangered – forest cutting aur hunting se.
Aakar: 60–90 cm tak lamba aur 7-10 kg wajan.

Dinosaur

Naam: Tyrannosaurus rex (T. Rex).
Awas: North America – 68–66 million saal pehle.
Aahar: Carnivorous – anya dinosaurs ka shikar karta tha.
Pehchaan: 12 meter lamba, 6 tonne wajan, majboot jaws, chhoti haath.
Vilupti: Asteroid impact aur uske baad ke paryavaranik badlaav se.
Visheshta: Fossil records ke anusar, sabse mashhoor dinosaur.

Gaur 🐃

Naam: Gaur (Indian Bison).
Awas: India, Nepal, Bhutan, Southeast Asia ke jungle.
Aahar: Herbivore – ghaas, daali, chhoti jhaadiyaan.
Pehchaan: Sabse bada jangli bovine – 2.2 meter uncha, 1000+ kg.
Status: Vulnerable – habitat loss aur hunting ke kaaran.
Visheshta: Shaktishaali aur samudaayik prani, aksar herds mein rehta hai.

Ring Tailed Lemur 🐒

Naam: Ring-Tailed Lemur.
Awas: Madagascar ke dry forest aur rocky areas.
Aahar: Fruits, flowers, insects, patte.
Pehchaan: Black-and-white ringed tail jo samvaad mein madad karta hai.
Vyavhaar: Diurnal aur samudaayik prani.
Status: Endangered – habitat destruction se sankat mein.

Malay Civet

Naam: Malay Civet.
Awas: Southeast Asia ke tropical rainforests.
Aahar: Omnivore – fruits, insects, chhoti janwaron ka shikar.
Pehchaan: Patli sharir, chhoti mooh aur khas sugandhit ras.
Status: Near Threatened – forest cutting se sankat.
Raat ka Jeevan: Nocturnal – raat mein active rehta hai.
